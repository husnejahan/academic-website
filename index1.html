About

I have been working as a Research Assistant for the past two years, focused on AI innovation in 3D vision, point cloud compression, and video super-resolution. I have a strong background in neural network research, specializing in 2D and 3D video super-resolution as well as point cloud data completion and compression. I am particularly strong in advancements in video understanding and generation technologies. One of my ongoing research projects involves developing a novel transformer-based approach for point cloud video completion (super-resolution). Additionally, I am working on a transformer model inspired by large language models (LLMs) to enhance immersive media compression for both 3D and 2D video super-resolution.
